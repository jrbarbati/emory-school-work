Variable Types
double	default type
	float or real
single 	float or real
	doesn't hold as large a number as double
int8, int16, int32, int64
	to find the range of numbers it can hold, just to 2^n where n is the number of bits
	the int can hold.
uint8, uint16, etc
	low is always 0
	high is always 2^n - 1
	uint8 holds numbers 0 - 255

Numerical Operators
Uniary and Binary
	Unary - acts on single operand (negation sign)
	Binary - acts on two operands (addition, division, etc)

> - greater than
< - less than
>= - greater than or equal to
<= - less than or equal to
== - equal to
~= - not equal to	

Logical Operators
&& - and
|| - or
~ - not
true shows as a 1 in MATLAB
false shows as a 0 in MATLAB
